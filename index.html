Workshop Overview

This workshop brings together researchers and practitioners from computer graphics, AI, cognitive science, and the arts to explore the many dimensions of drawing‚Äîhow people draw, how machines can draw, and how the two might draw together.

Our goal is to better understand drawing as both a cognitive process and a computational challenge, and to spark new ideas at the intersection of creativity, perception, and generative technologies.
